Square Dance

... Because I'm terrible at art.
A game made for Ludum Dare 31.
It's kind of reminiscent of old arcadey flash games.
Move mouse to move character, left click to dash/attack, right click for bomb when your meter is full. Hit 'm' to mute the music.
Credit goes to Craftyjs,for the game framework, and Ableton for the samples in the music.
